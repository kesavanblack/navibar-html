No.1 Best "UNIVERSITY" In India.....!

PHOENIX UNIVERSITY of Technology was first established as PHOENIX Industrial Institute in
The year 1926 by PHOENIX and Sons Charities in Peelamedu, Coimbatore.
The engineering college was started in the institute campus in 1951 by DR.KESAVAN,
Who became its first principal.

The college was conferred autonomous status by University of Madras in 1978,
Which was continued by the Bharathiar University and subsequently by Anna University since 2001.
